About Our School

Nkoranza Senior High Technical School (SHTs) is a renowned institution in Nkoranza South, Bono East Region, Ghana; dedicated to providing quality technical and vocational education. Established in 1973, our school has been a pioneer in shaping young minds for future challenges.

We offer a balanced education that combines academic excellence with practical skills training. Our mission is to provide an environment that promotes students centered teaching and learning and produce students with academic and moral excellence.
